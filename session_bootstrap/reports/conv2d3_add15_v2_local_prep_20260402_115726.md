## Target Rationale

- `fused_conv2d3_add15` is still a major runtime hotspot after the transpose-lane
  structural candidates were dropped on real hardware.
- The accepted `v1` line already removed the extra output pass by bias-fusing the
  epilogue; the dropped `P2` and `P4` conv2d3 micro-tunes showed that simply
  retuning reduction tiles or unroll depth is not enough.
- This `v2` keeps the accepted direct `lv347` access pattern, tile shape, and
  reduction split intact, but repacks the tiny `3x12x7x7` kernel into
  `kernel_pack(6,7,7,2,3)` so the innermost output-channel sweep reads three
  contiguous weights per `(rc_0, rc_1, ry, rx)` tuple on Cortex-A72.
- That is a narrower cache-locality change than the recently failed transpose
  structural rewrites: it does not fuse pad/dilate, does not add guarded direct
  reads, and does not alter the accepted output tile geometry.
